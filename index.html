The Challenge

The Current Bottleneck

Manual Tracking

  • Reliance on scattered paper logs and basic spreadsheets.
  • High risk of human error during peak business hours.
  • Difficulty in generating instant, accurate reports.

Operational Delays

  • Time-consuming manual stock audits.
  • Frequent stockouts or overstocking on critical tire models.
  • Slower customer service due to data uncertainty.
Currently, Jhaerin Tire Supply faces significant bottlenecks. They rely on manual logs, leading to errors and delays in stock auditing. This results in either missing out on sales due to stockouts or tying up capital in overstock.

Our Solution

Automated Inventory Intelligence

A custom-built, centralized digital platform designed specifically for the unique workflow of tire supply management.

Centralized Database

Unified record of every SKU, brand, and size across multiple branches.

Real-Time Updates

Instant reflection of sales and restock actions to maintain stock integrity.

Smart Analytics

Actionable insights into best-selling models and inventory health.

Our proposed solution is an Automated Inventory Intelligence platform. It centralizes all data, provides real-time updates, and offers smart analytics to help the business grow.

Staff Experience

Typical User Workflow

Restocking Process

  1. Login to Admin Dashboard
  2. Scan or Search for Tire SKU
  3. Input "Quantity Received"
  4. Instant Batch Total Update

Sales Transaction

  1. Staff verifies stock on-site
  2. Log Sale in System
  3. Auto-Deduct from Inventory
  4. Low stock alert (if triggered)
Here's how staff will use it. Restocking is a simple 4-step process, as is recording a sale. The goal is to make these interactions as fast as possible.

Data Protection

Security Architecture

Authentication

Multi-factor readiness and hashed credentials (BCrypt) to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive inventory records.

Data Integrity

Transaction locking ensures two staff members can't accidentally overwrite the same stock count simultaneously.

Built-in Compliance & safety

Security is baked in. We use industry-standard hashing for passwords and transaction locking to ensure inventory data remains perfectly accurate even with multiple users.
